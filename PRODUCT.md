# Product

## Register

product

## Users

African B2B operators (SMBs, enterprises, agencies) who use WhatsApp as their primary business communication channel. They are operators, customer service managers, and developers who need to automate conversations at scale. Context: managing high-volume WhatsApp inboxes, deploying AI agents for sales and support, generating quotes and documents directly from chat.

## Product Purpose

Nodes Flow is a WhatsApp messaging automation platform with AI agent management. It lets businesses connect their WhatsApp Business account, configure AI agents that respond to customer messages, generate PDF documents (quotes, receipts), and analyze conversation performance. Success = an operator who no longer touches their inbox manually.

## Brand Personality

Precise, powerful, Congolese-built. The dashboard is a tool for people who move fast and trust data. It is dark, sharp, and deliberate — not flashy.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS light-mode dashboards (Notion, Linear look-alikes)
- Overloaded analytics tools (multiple sidebar menus, nested tabs everywhere)
- Gradient-heavy fintech templates
- Consumer chat apps (WhatsApp/Slack visual clones)

## Design Principles

1. **Operator confidence** — every screen should feel like a cockpit, not a form. Data is legible at a glance.
2. **Yellow as signal, not decoration** — #FFCC00 marks actions and active state only. Not backgrounds, not borders at rest.
3. **Dark means focused** — the dark canvas (#0a0a0a / #121212) keeps the operator's attention on content, not chrome.
4. **Progressive disclosure** — show summary first, details on demand. No wall of settings on first load.
5. **African-built confidence** — no apologetic micro-copy. Direct, clear, zero buzzwords.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Framer Motion respects `prefers-reduced-motion`. All interactive elements have visible focus rings (yellow ring). Touch targets ≥ 44px.
